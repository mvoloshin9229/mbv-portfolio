New Girl's Final Season

For the final season of New Girl, I was tasked with finding a theme that would be instantly recognizable for die-hard fans and provide an in for longtime viewers that had dropped off at some point. I decided on a "Adulting to Adult" theme that would show the growth of the main characters throughout the years and focus on the fan favorite quotes/moments from years' past. We also ran a very successful Twitter activation where interested fans would get their picture placed in the frame just like Jess Day in the theme song.

The Gifted Season 1

To get comic fans excited for FOX's X-Men spin-off show, The Gifted, we were focused on making the followers feel like they were mutants. This activation started at San Diego Comic-Con where visitors were tested for the "mutant gene." We then launched an Instagram account for the shady government Sentinel Services where users would put their thumb on the middle of the grid and learn that they too had the mutant gene. This culminated in an Instagram/Facebook AR filter that would show you what mutant powers you received!
